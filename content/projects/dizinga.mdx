Dizinga is a platform where creators can upload their arts and easily sell merchandise for their audience.

During this project I worked closely with the frontend team to create the artist panel for Dizinga. My responsibilities were to create responsive design integrated with the logic to display the backend information about the user.

The artist panel includes overview for orders, possibility to create a new product and add it to the store, check and edit on previous designs and more.
