Bryant Dental is a UK-based manufacturer and distributor for dentistry equipment. The company's main focus is dental loupes, but they also sell headlights, cameras and other high-end products for dentists.

With a combination of 3d renders, smooth transitions and custom interactions we achieved a pleasant experience for the end user, that helped Bryant Dental provide the security of their brand to dentists and to be seen for their capability to innovate & design dental products. 

<VideoGifImg type="video" path="bryant-dental/video1.mp4"/> 

The presentational side of the website was built with GatsbyJs, a React-based open source framework for performance. As Frontend Lead I my responsibilities include: architecturing the project, creating amazing eye catching animations, integration with API and headless CMS from strapi. On top of the implementation side, I was coordinating a team of 5 frontend developers over a period of one and a half years.
