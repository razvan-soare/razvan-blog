Innovative Cycling Services Designed for Passionate Riders, HonorCycles offers Servicing, Shipping, Storage and marketplace for bikes.

A beautiful presentational project with an easy flow to book precise bike repairs or storage. 
A small summer project done together with my colleague on the backend, developing a system where people can service their bikes.
With custom images for each bike type and rapid booking process, the user flow could not be more intuitive and easy to use.
